What's inside

Everything You Need

A complete system built specifically for badminton tournament organizers. No generic event software.

Registration Management
Accept, review, and approve team registrations. Track payment status, send email confirmations, and manage cancellations — all from one screen.
Bracket Generation
Auto-generate round-robin groups with a single click. Shuffle seedings, manually drag teams between groups, and export printable brackets.
Smart Scheduling
Generate a full match schedule that respects court availability and automatically detects player conflicts across multiple levels.
Multi-Level Support
Run Level H, G, F, Open, Mixed, Women's — all simultaneously. Players registered in multiple levels are conflict-checked automatically.
Live Notifications
Get real-time alerts for new registrations, payment submissions, cancellation requests, and at-risk levels — with full history.
Export Anywhere
Export brackets, schedules, and registration lists to Excel or print-ready PDF. Share directly with players and venue staff.

Designed Around The Court

Unlike generic event tools, this system understands badminton — court slots, game durations, level formats, and how players move between matches. The scheduler knows that a player in Level H can't also be on Court 3 in Level G at the same time.

Dynamic court reduction rules (12 → 6 → 3 courts)
